Robert Greene

Robert Greene is an American author of books on mastery, strategy, power, war and seduction. He has written seven international bestsellers: The 48 Laws of Power, The Art of Seduction, The 33 Strategies of War, The 50th Law, Mastery, The Laws of Human Nature and The Daily Laws.

Why should you read the books of Robert Greene?

  • Learn to know and to control the emotions boiling within yourself
  • Learn how to influence, seduce people and avoid the traps of the Toxic Types
  • Learn the Laws of Power to navigate the social arena and avoid the traps
  • Learn how to deal with fear and conquer it
  • Learn how to identify your Life Task and achieve Mastery in your field
